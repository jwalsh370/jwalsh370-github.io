Who am I?

I am a creator, developer, writer, and artist.

I began humbly in New Orleans, and have since travelled the majority of the continental United States, as well as small sections of the world. I have found beauty in painful places and learned it well. Leading me here to you, not as who I was, but who I am currently, changing even in this moment. Using this blog as my medium, I will show you my love for the arts; music, movies, prose. You will see what I see, and be presented with what I hear.

This page also contains lists of my favorite things, with a Spotify playlist I have curated.



This site is broken down into five sections:

  1. Think: This is my main thought. Something I have been ruminating on, a philosophy that has been revealed to me, or just an interest of note. Like the Queen in chess this section allows me to roam the board with ease.
  2. See: This is where I show you what I see. A photo I have taken, art drawn by one of my talented friends, or maybe a cinematic scene that stood out to me.
  3. Hear: This is where you listen to what I hear. A song that has stuck with me, a speech that has changed me, or a poem recited.
  4. Learn: This is where I present new knowledge. The internet is overflowing with information, therefore, we should be constantly learning, this is my journey as a student of life.
  5. Create: This section is where I will present things I have created. A short story I have written, a game that I have made, an IPhone movie, or even a meal.
